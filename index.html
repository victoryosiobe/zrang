Zombie Ranger

Guide To Play

  • You get scores by having your bullets hit zombies
  • Just tap in the direction the zombies are coming to shoot
  • If they get to you, your health decreases by 10%
  • If you can get up to 100 in score, an auto shooter friend (AI) would be by your side, killing the zombies
